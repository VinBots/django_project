Instructions

Welcome to the input interface of Net0Tracker! The objective is to enable you input new data, correct existing data and upload supporting documents for each company in the database. To date, there are 6 types of possible inputs:

  1. General Info: upload various types of documents, like the sustainability report of the company or its TCFD report
  2. Disclosures: provide information related to GHG emissions reporting, coverage and verification
  3. Net Zero Claims: provide details about the net zero claims of the company, in terms of scope and timeline
  4. GHG Inventory: provide the full GHG inventory (all scopes, all scope 3 categories, location and market-based)
  5. Targets: provide the details of the GHG reduction targets, in terms of type of targets (gross/net, absolute/intensity), % reduction objectives, timeline, scope and baseline
  6. CDP - Climate Change Questionnaire: provide information about the CDP Climate Change questionnaire
In each section, please upload supporting documents (maximum 5 files by form) so that each data can be verified and referenced in the future.
