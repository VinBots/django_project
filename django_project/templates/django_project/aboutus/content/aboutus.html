About Us

I am French, 41 and work for a major energy mutlinational company (ENGIE), as the Chief Financial Officer of its sustainability consulting arm (ENGIE Impact). For many years, my goal has been to better connect finance with sustainability. During summer 2021, after reading a report from Yale University and observing some great initiatives from data scientists to track COVID cases, I embarked on a personal (crazy) project and studied the sustainability reporting of the top 100 largest US companies. I created this website after realizing that there was a lack of clear benchmarking of the environmental performance of companies whereas the number of net zero claims have exploded in the past 2 years. Even if it is incredibly challenging to distinguish real ambitions from marketing campaign (what our industry calls 'greenwashing'), it is possible.


I hope this website can contribute to better understand net zero claims and encourage companies to better disclose their carbon footprint and take actions for real to fight against climate change.
